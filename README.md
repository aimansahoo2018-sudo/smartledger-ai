# SmartLedger Enterprise AI

AI-powered fintech intelligence dashboard designed for predictive analytics, intelligent invoicing, financial forecasting, and enterprise-grade financial monitoring.

---

## Overview

SmartLedger Enterprise AI is a modern AI-native FinOps platform focused on transforming financial operations through intelligent analytics and automated insights.

The platform combines:
- Financial intelligence
- Predictive analytics
- AI-powered reporting
- Cash flow monitoring
- Tax intelligence
- Smart operational forecasting
